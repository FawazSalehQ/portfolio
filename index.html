§ 01 / Profile

Two disciplines, one practice.

I work at the intersection of organizational development and AI adoption. The place where workforce assessments, change management, and the tools the team uses next quarter all sit on the same page.

For the past four years I've built and delivered learning, training, and OD programs at BAE Systems Arabian Industries. Leading induction for thousands of learners, authoring procedural frameworks that passed CCB review. My proven leadership in leading training cycles with smooth procedures, as well as my attention to detail offering quality auditing solutions, have led me to evolve into thinking beyond the daily demands of my career.

Today my focus is AI adoption inside a regulated industrial environment: building local LLM solutions for safety and data residency, shipping Python dashboards and internal SaaS that have cut routine workload by 40%, and authoring the SOPs and concept-of-operation documents the new functions will run on.
